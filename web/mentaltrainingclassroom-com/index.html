The best athletes in the world use simple skills that our students can use to improve their performance in the classroom.

This is the 1st program in the world to utilize the proven strategies of elite athletes to improve the lives of students. Mental Skills teaches kids how to control their thoughts; which effects their emotions and ultimately, their actions. If they master these skills, they won't just be better students; they'll be better people.  

What does teaching Mental Skills Training have to do with students or the classroom? Middle school is a period of difficult transition for most kids. It can be filled with extreme variances in emotions which can affect how students handle every day situations. Students within this course are taught life skills to accomplish their dreams by not only setting achievable, measurable goals, but most importantly, RECOGNIZING THEIR THOUGHTS AS THEY HAPPEN.

Mission: This program gives teachers simple step by step ways to engage students in a process that trains them how to take charge of their life, their education and ultimately their future by becoming aware of their thoughts, setting goals and making a plan.

The unit content was adapted from MTI's Certified Mental Coach by Jane Enloe, MA ( Sequoia Middle School At Risk teacher)
